MARVEL networks

This graph shows a map of Marvel positionned close to those they are the most linked with.

The network includes all (writers and pencillers)(including teams) mentioned as involved in at least all comic book stories registered into Marvel's API (stories accounting more than were ignored; a few isolated sets of from the others were removed).

ClickTap the top buttons to switch between reduced or complete versions of the networks of characters or creators, using avatar pics or community colors.

The size of each node reflects how often the have been accounted as participating to stories within Marvel API's data.

Colors indicate which family* each character belongs to (, etc.).

Colors indicate whether a creator is mainly a , an or really a bit of .

Marvel API's data is not always perfect and some stories miss information, although it is quite rich and results look good enough!

ClickTap on the colored nodespictures to see each creatorcharacter's links and details.
