MARVEL networks

This graph shows a map of Marvel where each one is positionned close to those he/she is most linked with.

Click on the colored nodespictures to see their specific links and details.

The network includes all (writers and pencillers)(including teams and some alter-egos) mentioned as involved in at least all comic book stories registered within Marvel's API (stories accounting more than were ignored; a few isolated sets of from the others were removed).

The size of each node reflects how often the have been accounted as participating to stories within Marvel API's data.

Colors indicate which "family"* each character belongs to (, etc.).

Colors indicate whether a creator is mainly a writer, an artist or really a bit of both.

Marvel API's data is not always perfect and some stories miss information, although it is quite rich and results look good enough!

Click the top buttons to switch between the networks of
characters and creators,
their reduced or complete versions,
with avatar pics or community colors.

* Communities are automatically computed using the Louvain algorithm, so they are sometimes imprecise
